Token Allocation

Tokens are not a tool for personal gain. They are a mechanism to channel contributions toward impactful humanitarian work, operational sustainability, and mission continuity.

Allocation is strictly defined: a majority for relief programs, a portion for operational transparency, and a reserved fraction for ethical oversight. There is no profit distribution or personal enrichment.

Guiding Principles

  • All token movement serves humanity and mission purposes only.
  • No interest (Riba) or speculative gains are permitted.
  • Transparency and reporting ensure public trust at all times.
  • Founder has no financial privilege or ownership.
  • Structural safeguards prevent any misuse or diversion.

Transparency & Accountability

Detailed reports of token distribution, usage, and governance decisions are made publicly available. Every transaction is visible and aligns with the ethical framework of B2H.
