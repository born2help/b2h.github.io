B2H Constitution

This Constitution exists to protect the purpose, integrity, and humanity of the B2H project — now and forever.

B2H was created as a humanitarian initiative. It exists to help people in need, protect human dignity, and build systems that serve humanity rather than exploit it.

No success, popularity, or financial pressure may ever justify moving B2H away from this purpose.

1. Mission Lock

The mission of B2H is permanent and non-negotiable. It exists solely to serve humanity and reduce suffering.

No individual, group, or authority — including the founder — may alter this mission for personal, political, or financial gain.

Any action that contradicts this mission is considered a violation of the Constitution.

2. Founder Safeguard

The founder serves as a guardian of the mission, not an owner of the project.

Founder authority exists only to: protect integrity, ensure transparency, and prevent exploitation or deviation.

The founder may not use B2H for personal enrichment or excessive benefit.

All founder-related resources are limited to reasonable operational and representation needs, strictly aligned with the mission.

3. Use of Funds

All funds, assets, and resources connected to B2H exist for three purposes only:

  • Humanitarian assistance and aid
  • Operational sustainability
  • Mission protection and growth

No funds may be used for luxury, excess, or exploitation.

4. Transparency

Transparency is a foundational principle of B2H.

As the project grows, summaries, disclosures, and reports will be shared publicly to maintain trust.

Openness is not optional — it is a responsibility.

5. Permanence

This Constitution exists to outlive individuals.

If leadership changes, the mission, safeguards, and principles must remain intact.

B2H must never become a tool for the enrichment of a few while others suffer.

This Constitution stands as a promise — to participants, to the vulnerable, and to future generations — that B2H will always choose humanity first.
