Mission Lock

B2H is permanently committed to serving humanity. All burns, treasury actions, and governance decisions must prioritize helping the poor, the needy, and underserved communities worldwide.

The founder is ethically protected to ensure the sustainability of leadership, family responsibilities, and global humanitarian coordination without compromising the integrity of charitable funds.
