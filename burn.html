How Burns Help Humanity

In many projects, burning tokens is done only to increase price. In B2H, burning exists for something far greater.

Every burn represents a conscious sacrifice — a decision to reduce supply so that value can be redirected toward helping real human beings.

A portion of every burn is allocated to humanitarian support: food, shelter, medical help, and emergency assistance for families who are struggling to survive.

Another portion strengthens the project itself — ensuring that B2H can continue operating, expanding, and serving people without dependence or exploitation.

This balance allows B2H to grow responsibly: helping people today, while protecting the mission for tomorrow.

Burns are not destruction. They are transformation — turning participation into dignity, and value into mercy.

When you participate in B2H, you are not only part of a system — you are part of a service.
