About the Founder

I am not a corporation, a venture capitalist, or a billionaire. I am a human being who has seen pain, struggle, and injustice, and I chose to dedicate the rest of my life to helping others.

At the age of 56, I understand that life is temporary. What remains is intention, honesty, and the impact we leave behind. B2H was created not for wealth, but for responsibility.
