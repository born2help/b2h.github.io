About the Founder

I was not born with wealth, influence, or security. I was born into the same world many of you know ‚Äî a world where survival comes before dreams.

I have lived long enough to understand something painful: most suffering is invisible. People do not cry loudly. They endure quietly.

I have watched parents smile in front of their children while their hearts were breaking inside. I have seen capable people lose hope, not because they were weak, but because help never arrived.

I am 56 years old. Time has taught me that life does not ask who deserves pain. It simply gives it. And one day, I realized: if I remain silent, I become part of the injustice.

This project was not created to make me important. It was created because I could no longer ignore the quiet suffering happening all around us.

B2H exists because someone must choose to care ‚Äî not once, not for attention, but every day, for as long as they are alive.

I do not promise perfection. I promise sincerity. I promise that every decision will be weighed against one simple question: does this help a human being?

If one day I am gone, let this project continue with the same purpose: to protect dignity, to reduce suffering, and to remind the world that humanity still matters.

Mission Lock

B2H was created for one reason only: to help human beings in need.

This mission is permanent. It cannot be changed by profit, popularity, pressure, or power.

No individual, including the founder, has the right to redirect B2H away from serving humanity. Leadership exists only as responsibility, never as ownership.

All systems, funds, and decisions must answer one question: does this reduce human suffering? If the answer is no, it must not be done.

If one day this project grows beyond its founder, this mission must remain untouched. B2H must never exist to enrich a few while many suffer.

This mission lock stands as a promise ‚Äî to the poor, to the forgotten, and to future generations ‚Äî that B2H will always choose humanity first.

Transparency & Use of Funds

Trust is sacred. B2H understands that people do not give blindly ‚Äî they give when they feel respected and protected.

Every resource connected to B2H exists for a clear purpose: to help people in need, to sustain humanitarian operations, and to ensure this mission can continue for generations.

Funds are allocated with responsibility and restraint, including humanitarian aid, operational sustainability, outreach, and the basic functioning required to serve people globally.

No funds exist for luxury, excess, or personal enrichment. Leadership expenses are limited to what is necessary to carry the mission responsibly, not to profit from it.

Transparency is not a promise for later ‚Äî it is a principle from the beginning. As B2H grows, records, summaries, and disclosures will be shared openly to maintain public trust.

Every decision involving funds must pass one test: does this protect dignity, reduce suffering, and strengthen our ability to help? If not, it must not proceed.
