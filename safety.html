Read This First

Life teaches us something slowly: good intentions are not enough. Responsibility matters.

This project exists to help humanity ‚Äî not to create financial stress, false hope, or pressure on anyone.

Who Should Participate

Participation is meant only for those who can do so calmly, without harming their family, without sacrificing necessities, and without borrowing or pressure.

Who Should Not Participate

If participation would:

  • Cause hardship or debt
  • Create emotional pressure or fear
  • Replace basic family needs
  • Be done out of desperation

Then it should not be done.

No Promises of Certainty

B2H does not promise guaranteed returns, instant wealth, or risk-free outcomes.

Anyone claiming otherwise does not speak for this project.

Conscience Over Numbers

Growth that harms people is not success.

If B2H ever grows large, it must still protect the smallest participant.

A Personal Note

This project was shaped by years of observing how easily hope can be misused.

B2H chooses restraint where others choose hype. Because some lessons are learned only with time.
