No Guaranteed Returns

No Pressure, No Hype

Activities such as manipulation, false claims, insider advantage, or exploitative behavior are rejected and discouraged.

Voluntary & Informed Participation

Every participant is responsible for their own decisions. B2H encourages understanding, patience, and ethical alignment before any involvement.

Our Commitment

The mission will never be sacrificed for growth, popularity, or money. If protecting people requires slowing down, we choose to slow down.

Who Should Participate

Participation is meant only for those who can do so calmly, without harming their family, without sacrificing necessities, and without borrowing or pressure.

Who Should Not Participate

Conscience Over Numbers

Growth that harms people is not success. Even if B2H grows large, it must still protect the smallest participant.

A Personal Note
