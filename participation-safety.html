Founder Safeguards

The Founder of B2H carries the mission as amanah (trust), ensuring it cannot be hijacked for personal gain or lost to external pressures.

Founder Responsibility

Authority exists solely to protect purpose, ensure continuity, and uphold accountability.

Founder Continuity & Protection

The Founder cannot be removed, overridden, or sidelined by any individual or group as long as he remains capable and ethically aligned with the Constitution.

Succession Through Trust

Authority transfers only to individuals personally designated by the Founder. No group, vote, or committee may override this safeguard.
